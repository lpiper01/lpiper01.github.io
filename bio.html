Louis Piper Carson
Tufts Class of 2020
BSCS

Early Life

Louis was born February 13th, 1998 in Mount Auburn hospital to parents Shawn Piper and Lisa Carson. Raised in Medford, Massachusetts, he attended Mystic Valley Regional Charter School in Malden.

A member of the National Honor Society during High School, Louis enjoys volunteering at St. Luke's in Chelsea, where he has been serving meals to the underserved since 2013.

He was accepted into Tufts University in late 2015, where he continues to study until his expected graduation in May 2020.

Notable Events

  • September 2003: Began school at MVRCS
  • December 2015: Was accepted to Tufts University
  • May 2016: Graduated from MVRCS
